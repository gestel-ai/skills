# Template Catalog

GESTEL starts with 6 creative families and 3 layouts per family. Keep the catalog small until review quality, Korean copy, product preservation, and performance learning are stable.

## First-Pack Default

- Select 3 recommended families.
- Pick 1 layout per family.
- Generate 1 image per layout in 4:5.
- Generate 1:1 and 9:16 only after the user selects candidate variants.

## Families

| Family | Job | Required inputs | Typical angle | Review emphasis |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `review_proof` | Make proof visible quickly | Product photo, review or rating evidence, claim evidence ID | Trust, social proof, buyer language | Claim evidence and quote accuracy |
| `problem_solution` | Connect purchase pain to product use | Product photo, problem statement, benefit claim | Problem relief, before/after without unsupported transformation | Product fidelity and softened claims |
| `limited_offer` | Create a time or price reason to act | Product photo, price or discount evidence, offer deadline | Coupon, bundle, sale, shipping benefit | Price evidence, deadline evidence, disclosure |
| `comparison_grid` | Compare alternatives or choices | Product photo, comparison basis, allowed competitor or category wording | Why this product over common alternative | Evidence-backed comparison and no unverifiable superlatives |
| `ingredient_callout` | Explain material, ingredient, or function | Product photo, ingredient or feature evidence | Feature clarity, functional benefit | Regulated claims and product accuracy |
| `marketplace_thumbnail` | Win fast recognition in a feed or listing | Product photo, product name, one core benefit | Product recognition, category cue, simple hook | Text readability and safe zone |

## Layout Count

Each family should have exactly 3 quality-controlled layouts before broad expansion:

1. `focus`: single product hero, minimal copy.
2. `proof`: product plus evidence, review, rating, or badge.
3. `compare`: product plus contrast, bundle, ingredient, or use-case panel.

The layout names are stable handles, not visual design requirements. A specific template may use a more precise internal ID such as `review_proof.proof.v1`.

## Recommendation Rules

- Start with `review_proof` when credible reviews or ratings are confirmed.
- Start with `limited_offer` when price, discount, shipping, bundle, or deadline evidence is confirmed.
- Start with `ingredient_callout` only when the feature or ingredient is supported by the product URL or uploaded evidence.
- Use `problem_solution` for broad pain-point products, but soften unsupported outcome claims.
- Use `comparison_grid` only when the comparison basis is specific and defensible.
- Use `marketplace_thumbnail` when the product photo needs fast category recognition or the offer is simple.

## Freshness

Meta and Instagram specs can change. Before making a permanent platform claim, verify live documentation or add a dated reference with source and refresh trigger. This evergreen catalog only defines GESTEL's local template contract.
