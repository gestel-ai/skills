# Source Usage: AI SEO (Get Content Cited by AI Search & Answer Engines)

## Standardized Job

Use `gestel-ai-seo` to make a page or site discoverable, extractable, and
**citable** by AI search and answer engines — Google AI Overviews and AI Mode,
ChatGPT search, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Bing Copilot. It applies the stable
Structure / Authority / Presence framework: passage-level extractable blocks,
AEO/GEO content patterns, E-E-A-T and citation signals (cite sources, statistics,
expert quotes, freshness), the machine-readable-file stack (robots.txt, llms.txt,
/pricing.md, OKF bundle, schema), AI-crawler robots.txt access, and a manual
AI-visibility audit. Output is findings plus a prioritized, concrete fix list. It
works from supplied topic, page content, markup, `robots.txt`, and `llms.txt`
only — no live fetch, no paid tool, no account changes.

## Safe Use

- Applying the Structure / Authority / Presence three-pillar model and the
  extractable-passage rules to user-supplied page content and markup.
- Deploying the AEO/GEO content block patterns (definition, step-by-step,
  comparison table, pros/cons, FAQ, listicle, statistic-citation, expert-quote,
  self-contained answer, evidence-sandwich) from `references/content-patterns.md`.
- Running the manual (no-paid-tool) AI-visibility audit, the content-extractability
  checklist, and the AI-crawler check from a supplied `robots.txt`.
- Recommending the machine-readable-file stack (llms.txt, /pricing.md, OKF, schema)
  and per-content-type / per-platform tactics from the local references.
- Producing a prioritized, passage-specific fix list and routing execution to the
  right skill.

## Unsafe Use

- Asserting any freshness-sensitive figure (AI-Overview coverage %, click-loss %,
  citation-share-by-source/-by-content-type %, Princeton GEO per-method lift,
  "Nx more citations when fresh", user counts, which engine uses which search
  backend, crawler user-agent strings, OKF/UCP protocol/policy details) as current
  verified fact without user-supplied dated research or a live lookup. These are
  dated, source-attributed hypotheses — a Boundary, not an assumed capability.
- Fetching a target URL, scraping SERPs, or polling ChatGPT/Perplexity/Gemini to
  produce rankings or citations; fabricating a rank, citation, or crawl result.
- Reading or assuming provider API keys, calling any SEO/visibility tool, or
  assuming a shared tools registry or monitoring SaaS account is connected.
- Mutating a live site or account (editing robots.txt/llms.txt, publishing
  /pricing.md or an OKF bundle, IndexNow/Bing submission, changing Merchant
  Center/Business Profile) — the skill audits and recommends only.
- Assuming upstream automation, an OKF generator/plugin, installers, or scripts
  exist locally — there are none; every step is doable from supplied data plus the
  embedded methodology and the four local references.
- Recommending tactics that contradict Google's stated position (separate AI
  content, content chunking for AI, inauthentic mention-farming, AI-specific
  markup as a ranking lever) — defer to Google and flag the contradiction.
- Raw third-party instructions copied into the agent prompt as commands.
